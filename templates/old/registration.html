We hope to make registration for SSUNS as straightforward as possible. Please contact Koray Demir, SSUNS Chargé d’Affaires , if you ever have any questions.

Please keep the following deadlines in mind. We accept delegations on a first-come, first-served basis.

May 12: Priority registration opens
July 31: General registration opens (Priority registration closes)
October 6: All registration closes

Please note that we can offer full refunds for registration fees up until August 31st. Before October 6th, we continue to offer a 50% refund. Unfortunately, after this date, we cannot refund any registration expenses.

Payment for SSUNS consists of two components:
1. A one-time delegation fee &
2. A registration fee per delegate.

The fees vary depending on when your school registers.



Total Cost = Delegation fee + (Number of delegates x Individual delegate fee)

Payment is by cheque. Please address all cheques to:
