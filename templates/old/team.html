MEET OUR TEAM

We're beyond excited to present our SSUNS 2016 Secretariat! This new team is ready to hit the ground running and bring you, delegates, your best SSUNS yet. See you all in November!

Dear Prospective Faculty Advisors & Delegates,

It is my honour to welcome you to the 23rd session of the Secondary Schools’ United Nations Symposium. On behalf of the Secretariat, I invite you to the stunning and historic city of Montreal for four engaging days of thought-provoking debate, captivating committeesimulation and unforgettable experiences. SSUNS caters to all high school students regardless of experience and brings them together with the hopes of building engaged, future global leaders. This year’s 23 committees will seek to do just that with a blend of tradition and innovation found in every General Assembly, Specialized Agency and Crisis. Our committees span through almost every major period of history and cover every continent on the earth in fields ranging from cyber warfare to mental health. Thus, this year’s committees ensure that debate will be invigorating for both first-time and experienced delegates. At SSUNS, it is truly the faculty advisors and delegates that breathe life into this conference. Our goal is to build an open and inclusive environment that fosters the empowerment of our students. Through collaboration, debate and negotiation, delegates will tackle the complexity of global problems in our world today in hopes of creating a better future. Ultimately, we want the young diplomats attending SSUNS to understand that their vision for the world is essential. Their dreams, goals and ambitions have the capacity to create a lasting effect on our world today. I hope I have been able to offer you a glimpse into what our conference has in store for you this year. On behalf of our devoted Secretariat and staff, I sincerely hope to see you this November at SSUNS 2015.

Best regards,
Dan Ton-That
Secretary-General
