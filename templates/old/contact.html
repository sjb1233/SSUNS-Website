SSUNS 2015 will take place at Hotel Bonaventure, located at 900 Rue de la Gauchetière O, Montréal, QC H5A 1E4, Canada

VENUE

Hotel Bonaventure Montreal is located in the heart of downtown Montreal. The hotel is home to premier conference facilities specializing in hosting large and small meetings, exhibitions and conventions. With 395 bedrooms and 2.5 acres of stunning landscaped rooftop gardens, The Hotel Bonaventure Montreal offers choice of garden or city skyline views. Swim all year round in the outdoor heated rooftop pool and enjoy a refreshing cool drink in summer at poolside bar.
