Registration

Registration Information

We hope to make registration as straightforward as possible.
Please contact Wendy Zhang, SSUNS Chargée d'Affaires, at schools@ssuns.org if you ever have any questions.


Registration Dates

Priority Registration

Registration will be open from May 12 - July 30.

Regular Registration

Registration will be open on July 31.

Registration will officially close on October 7. However, we will be opening the waitlist registration on a first-come first-serve basis (if capacity permits).

Payment Methods
Online (Paypal)

Please note that there is a 3% convenience fee for online payments, due to a fee charged to us by PayPal.


By Mail (Cheque)

A cheque made out to IRSAM (International Relations Students' Association of McGill) must be received at: IRSAM Inc.; 3480 Rue McTavish; Montreal, QC, Canada H3A 1X9

If you have any questions please contact our Chargee D’Affaires, Wendy Zhang, at schools@ssuns.org
Registration Fees

Priority Registration
A one-time, non-refundable fee of $90 per delegation (regardless of delegation size.)
+
A fee of $90 per delegate, tax included.

Regular Registration
A one-time, non-refundable fee of $95 per delegation (regardless of delegation size.)
+
A fee of $95 per delegate, tax included.

Please note that both American and overseas schools pay in USD and Canadian schools pay in Canadian dollars.

Refund Policy
Please note that we can offer full refunds* for registration fees up until August 31st. Before October 6th, we continue to offer a 50% refund*. Unfortunately, after this date, we cannot refund any registration expenses.

*Note: The refund does not include the one-time delegation fee
