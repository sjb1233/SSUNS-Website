The History behind our Conference


It began with a simple idea.

The hope was to create a place for high school students to explore the scope of global issues around them and to offer an opportunity for these students to take part as active young leaders. The reality has gone above and beyond the initial vision for Secondary Schools’ United Nations Symposium, and has only continued to expand and innovate in every possible capacity ever since.


For those new to our Conference


The Secondary Schools' United Nations Symposium (SSUNS) is a Model United Nations conference in Montreal, Quebec. Our venue is the Hotel Bonaventure Montreal, and our conference will be from November 10th - 13th of this year.

Please refer to the Registration page for information regarding fees, for the refund policy, and for the registration form and the Committee page for the list of committees at SSUNS 2016.

McGill Students for UNICEF


Representing UNICEF on campus, our group aims to raise funds and awareness for children’s issues in innovative and engaging ways that promote community on campus and globally.
